<h1 align="center">⚓ Eversea ⚓</h1>

**Eversea** is a hybrid Fishing RPG and Naval Combat game developed for a game jam under the theme **"The Calm Before the Storm".** By day, navigate serene waters to hunt for rare catches and manage your grid-based inventory. By night, defend the island and your ship from relentless pirate raids.

<h1 align="center">✨ Key Features ✨</h1>


#### Fishing & Exploration
* **Naval Maneuvering:** Take the helm of your ship and explore the open sea to find the best fishing spots.
* **Fishing Minigames:** Engaging mechanics to hook and reel in various aquatic species.
* **Tetris-Style Inventory:** Forget standard lists. You must strategically rotate and fit your catches into a limited hold—every square inch counts!
* **Island Economy:** Sell your bounty at the local island to fund your survival.

#### Combat & Upgrades
* **Ship Progression:** Invest gold to upgrade your ship’s speed, HP, and firepower.
* **Weaponry:** Start with a main cannon and unlock devastating side cannons to broaden your combat capabilities.
* **Nightly Sieges:** Survive the night as pirates attempt to pillage both your ship and the island.
